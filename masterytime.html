How Long Would it Take YOU to Master Something?

In the book "Outliers" by Malcolm Gladwell, he theorized that it would take around 10,000 hours of practice to achieve mastery in a field. By inputting your numbers below, you can figure out how long, based on his theory, it would take you to master something.

Your Time Will Appear Here

This program was made by Susie Renjith on June 25th, 2016.


A Small Side Note

This was the second project I created that included JavaScript. I wondered how long it would take to master something based on Malcom Gladwell's theory, so I created this engine, which calculates the time it would take one to master something based on the number of hours they practice a day and the number of days they practice a week.
